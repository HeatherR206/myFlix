Welcome to myFlix

Are you a movie enthusiast looking for a way to track your favorite films?

Or maybe you're obsessed with the work of a specific director or actor?

Then you'll love myFlix!

With myFlix, you can:

  • retrieve accurate information about your favorite movies, directors, actors, and more;
  • create and manage multiple watchlists, like "My Favorite Movies" or "Must Watch";
  • rate and review films you've seen;
  • receive personalized movie recommendations based on your viewing history

Sign up is free, quick, and simple!
